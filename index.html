About Me

I've worked in the banking industry for a couple of years. While I love what I've done and everything that I've learned. I decided it was time for a change. I'm a curious one so, I enjoy learning new things, creating new things, and especially something that is going to challenge me.

I began my coding adventure with Thinkful and I couldn't be happier. I've met and learned a lot from my peers and my mentor. I'm better prepared to becoming a software developer and I couldn't be more excited!

When I'm not coding for fun then I'm spending my spare time with family and friends, co-workers, or my dog Bella. I enjoy playing video games, going for long walks with Bella or runs, wine tasting, food tasting, taking road trips, or even just lying around the house doing nothing.

If you're fresh on the scene or just looking to hire a new developer, feel free to reach out to me. I'm excited to meet and work with other developers.
